## 🚀 Welcome!

Have you ever wondered...

- ❓ Why do some people remain calm under pressure while others panic?
- ❓ Why do intelligent people sometimes make poor decisions?
- ❓ Why do we procrastinate even when we know what to do?
- ❓ Why do some individuals inspire others effortlessly?
- ❓ Why do some people bounce back from failure while others give up?

If you've ever asked yourself these questions...

**Welcome to Behavioural Psychology.**

This repository is more than lecture notes.

It is a journey towards understanding yourself, improving your mindset, developing emotional intelligence, and becoming a better engineer, professional, and human being.

---

# 🎯 Course Vision

This course is designed to help engineering students understand **how the human mind works**, how behaviour influences success, and how psychological principles can improve personal and professional life.

Instead of memorizing theories, we focus on:

- 🧠 Self Discovery
- 💡 Better Thinking
- ❤️ Emotional Intelligence
- 🤝 Human Relationships
- 🚀 Personal Growth
